## Docs
SimpleReport is a free tool that helps COVID-19 testing sites report results. Designed in partnership with the CDC, SimpleReport makes it easy to record and report the results of rapid tests.

With real-time reporting and automatic formatting to match your public health departmentâ€™s requirements, SimpleHealth can help save your team time and increase the accuracy of your data.
